# Homepage Audit

## Website
https://www.clientfirstinvestments.com.au/

## Goal of the homepage
Convert visitors into consultation bookings.

## What works
- Clear financial advisory positioning
- Free consultation offer
- Services listed

## Problems
- Headline is too broad
- Target audience not clearly defined
- Weak conversion structure
- CTA could be stronger
- Trust signals not prominent enough

## Improvements needed
1. Rewrite hero section
2. Clarify who the service is for
3. Strengthen CTA
4. Improve section flow
## Key Conversion Problems

### 1. Headline is too broad
The homepage headline does not clearly identify the target audience or the specific result the visitor will get.

### 2. Value proposition is not strong enough
The homepage does not quickly explain why someone should choose the business.

### 3. CTA needs to be stronger
The call-to-action exists, but it is not reinforced enough throughout the page.

### 4. Service messaging is too general
The services are visible, but the benefits and outcomes are not clear enough.

### 5. Homepage structure can guide visitors better
The page should lead visitors more clearly from problem to solution to action.
