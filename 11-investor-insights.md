# Investor Insights Page

## Project Summary
The Investor Insights page was created for Client First Investments to transform Facebook ad creatives into educational website content.

The goal was to make paid ad content more valuable by turning it into a reusable website asset that builds trust and supports conversions.

---

## What I Did
- Planned the page structure in Wix
- Created a consistent layout for current and future ad creatives
- Reframed Facebook ads into educational content blocks
- Added CTA sections to guide users toward consultation bookings
- Positioned the page as a trust-building content hub rather than an ad gallery

---

## Why It Matters
Instead of relying only on social media platforms, this approach allows the business to keep valuable campaign content on its own website.

This improves:
- content longevity
- trust and authority
- website depth
- lead generation potential
