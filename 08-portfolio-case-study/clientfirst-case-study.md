# Client First Investments Website Optimization

## Objective
Improve website clarity and lead generation capability.

## Problems Identified
Broad messaging
Weak conversion structure
Unclear audience targeting

## Strategy
Rewrite homepage messaging
Improve service page structure
Strengthen calls to action

## Work Performed
Website audit
Copywriting improvements
Conversion optimization

## Outcome
Improved website positioning and clearer value proposition.
## Homepage Optimization – Phase 1

### Changes Made
• Rewrote the homepage headline  
• Added clearer supporting text under the headline  
• Updated the main CTA to "Book Your Free Consultation"  
• Changed the "Who are we?" section to "Who We Help"  
• Updated the text to focus on families and financial outcomes

### Goal
Improve clarity of the homepage messaging and make it immediately clear who the service is for and what results clients can expect.

### Result
The homepage now communicates the offer more clearly and better guides visitors toward booking a consultation.
