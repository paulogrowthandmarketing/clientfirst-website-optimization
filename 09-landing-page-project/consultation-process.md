# Consultation Process Section

## Objective

The consultation process section explains what visitors can expect after booking a consultation.

Clearly explaining the process helps reduce hesitation and increases the likelihood that visitors will complete the booking form.

---

## Section Title

Our Simple Process

---

## Process Steps

Step 1 – Initial Consultation  
Visitors book a free consultation to discuss their current financial situation and goals.

Step 2 – Strategy Review  
The Client First Investments team reviews the client's financial position and explores property investment opportunities.

Step 3 – Personalised Investment Plan  
A tailored property investment strategy is developed to help the client build long-term financial security.
