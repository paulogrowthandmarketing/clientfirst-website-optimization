# Hero Section Implementation

## Objective

The Hero section introduces the landing page and clearly communicates the primary offer: a free property investment consultation.

The goal is to immediately capture visitor attention and encourage them to book a consultation.

---

## Headline

Build Wealth Through Strategic Property Investment

---

## Subheadline

Book a free 60-minute consultation and discover how to reduce tax, pay off your mortgage faster, and build long-term wealth.

---

## Call to Action

Primary Button:

Book Your Free Consultation

Purpose:
Encourage visitors to scroll to the consultation form and book an appointment.

---

## Design Approach

The landing page was built using the existing website design to maintain brand consistency with the Client First Investments website.

The Hero section includes:

• Large headline  
• Clear value proposition  
• Prominent consultation button  
• Consistent background styling
