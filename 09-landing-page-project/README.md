# Client First Investments – Landing Page Project

## Project Goal

Create a high-converting landing page to generate consultation bookings for Client First Investments through Google Ads and other marketing campaigns.

---

## Landing Page Objective

Encourage visitors to book a **free property investment consultation**.

---

## Target Audience

Individuals or families interested in:

• Property investment  
• Reducing tax through property strategies  
• Paying off their mortgage faster  
• Building long-term wealth

---

## Funnel Structure

Traffic Source  
Google Ads / Search Traffic

↓

Landing Page

↓

Consultation Booking Form

↓

Phone consultation with Client First Investments

---

## Page Structure

Hero Section  
Clear value proposition and consultation offer

What You Will Learn

Simple 3-step process
