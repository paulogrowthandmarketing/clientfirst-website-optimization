# Reused Website Sections

## Objective

To maintain design consistency with the main Client First Investments website, existing sections were reused on the landing page.

This ensures a consistent brand experience for visitors arriving from advertising campaigns.

---

## Our Simple Process Section

The existing "Our Simple Process" section from the homepage was reused to explain how the consultation works.

This helps visitors clearly understand what happens after they book their consultation.

---

## Testimonials Section

The testimonials section was reused to provide social proof and increase visitor confidence.

Positive client feedback helps reinforce trust and credibility for new visitors.

---

## Design Strategy

Reusing existing sections allowed the landing page to maintain the same visual style, layout, and branding as the main website while reducing development time.
